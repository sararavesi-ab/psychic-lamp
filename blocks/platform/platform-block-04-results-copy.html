Results & Analytics

See what motivates your population — across every need.

The Results dashboard surfaces motivation intelligence across your entire organization. Compare motivational profiles across groups, track how drivers shift over time, and identify where specific needs are strongest or most unmet. Every data point maps back to the 12-need AgileBrain framework — so results are always interpretable, not just visible.

  • Population-level motivation comparison across all 12 needs
  • Filter by deployment, date range, group, or segment
  • Difference ranking to identify where needs diverge most
  • Export to PDF for reporting and stakeholder communication
