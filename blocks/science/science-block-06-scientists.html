The Scientists

The science is only as strong as the people behind it. AgileBrain's framework was built, tested, published, and refined by researchers whose careers are rooted in motivational science.

J. David Pincus, Ph.D.

Chief Scientist, AgileBrain

Dr. Pincus is the architect of the Motivational Intelligence Framework and the originator of the image-based methodology at its core. His research spans 30+ years, encompassing employee engagement, organizational motivation, and the neuroscience of human decision-making. He has published extensively in peer-reviewed journals and presented at international conferences on motivation, communication psychology, and workplace behavior.

William Malen, Ph.D.

Chief Scientist

Dr. Malen brings deep expertise in behavioral psychology and organizational research to the AgileBrain team. His work focuses on the validation and application of motivation intelligence in real-world settings — from workforce development and healthcare to education and performance coaching. His research underpins the empirical rigor behind AgileBrain's assessment framework.
