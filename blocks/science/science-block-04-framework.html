The Framework

The Motivation Intelligence Model

AgileBrain's framework maps motivation across three macro-dimensions and 12 distinct motivational needs. Every known derivation of human motivation, emotional state, or team dynamic maps to one of these Triads — both the presence and absence of a need can be detected, quantified, and interpreted.

The three levels — Foundational, Experiential, and Aspirational — represent the fundamental arenas of human striving. Each level contains four specific needs that can be independently measured and tracked over time.

The framework is the intelligence layer that makes the highest-signal output possible. Without it, data becomes statistics. With it, it becomes motivation intelligence.
